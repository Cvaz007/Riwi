About us

History and Origin

A few years ago, a passionate group of football fans found themselves sharing their love for the beautiful game in every corner of the world. These friends, each hailing from a different country, yearned for a way to unite fans and keep them informed about the most exciting events in international football. The idea of "The Field Informs" was born during a lively meeting where these enthusiasts discussed the challenges they faced in obtaining up-to-date and detailed information about their favorite leagues and teams. It was then that the vision of creating a unique space where football lovers could easily access the latest results, follow live matches, and share the excitement of the game with a global community emerged. Armed with their passion for football and a shared desire to provide a unique experience to other fans, the group embarked on the challenge of building "The Field Informs." Although they initially faced technological limitations, their determination led them to develop a simple yet impactful website, using only HTML and CSS, with the mission of making football information accessible to everyone, from avid fans to newcomers to the sport. Today, "The Field Informs" continues to grow thanks to the effort and dedication of the team behind the scenes. This project, which began as a shared dream, has now become a meeting point for football lovers worldwide, offering a unique experience that celebrates the diversity of teams and the excitement of the game.

Mission and Vision

Mission

Provide football enthusiasts with a centralized and accessible platform that offers accurate, timely, and relevant information about the results, events, and protagonists of the world of the beautiful game. We seek to foster passion for football by keeping our audience informed and connected to the excitement of the game.

Vision

We aspire to be the preferred source of global football information, standing out for our integrity, innovation, and commitment to quality. We aim to build a vibrant virtual community where fans find inspiration, enjoy quality content, and celebrate the diversity of football
